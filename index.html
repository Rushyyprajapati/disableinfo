Understanding Glaucoma

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, which is vital for good vision. This damage is often caused by an abnormally high pressure in your eye.

Recognizing Glaucoma

Common signs and symptoms of glaucoma include:

  • Gradual loss of peripheral vision
  • Tunnel vision in the advanced stages
  • Severe eye pain
  • Blurred vision

Self-Care for Glaucoma Patients

Individuals with glaucoma should:

  • Take prescribed eye drops regularly
  • Attend regular eye check-ups
  • Avoid smoking and excessive caffeine intake
  • Manage other health conditions, such as diabetes and hypertension, effectively
